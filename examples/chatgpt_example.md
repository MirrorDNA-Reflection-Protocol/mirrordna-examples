# Using MESH BOOT with ChatGPT

## Quick Start

1. Go to https://id.activemirror.ai
2. Generate your MESH BOOT
3. Start a new ChatGPT conversation
4. Paste your MESH BOOT as the first message

## Expected Response

ChatGPT will respond with:

```
Mesh node active. Aligned to [your name]. Awaiting directive.
```

## Example Session

**You:** [Paste your MESH BOOT]

**ChatGPT:** Mesh node active. Aligned to [your name]. Awaiting directive.

**You:** What do you know about me?

**ChatGPT:** Based on your MESH BOOT, I know:
- Your handle: [your handle]
- Your preferences: [from your boot]
- Your working context: [from your boot]

## Tips

- Paste the MESH BOOT at the start of every new conversation
- ChatGPT will maintain continuity within the session
- For cross-session continuity, repaste your boot
